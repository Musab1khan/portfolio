Frequently Asked Questions

How is ERPNext different from Odoo?

ERPNext is 100% open source with no licensing fees, includes all modules out of the box, offers unlimited users, and provides faster implementation with lower total cost of ownership.

Can ERPNext be hosted on our own server?

Yes, ERPNext can be self-hosted on your own servers, private cloud, or you can choose our cloud hosting solution. We support Docker, Kubernetes, and traditional server deployments.

Is ERPNext suitable for large enterprises?

Absolutely! ERPNext is scalable and used by companies with thousands of users. It supports multi-company, multi-location, and complex organizational structures.
